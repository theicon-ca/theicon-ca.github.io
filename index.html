Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

No appointments necessary! Just walk in anytime during our business hours. We'll be ready to help you.

How much is a diagnostic?

We offer free diagnostics and transparent, up-front quotes. You'll know the exact cost of the repair before we begin any work.

What brands do you repair?

We repair all brands of PCs and laptops, including Dell, HP, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, and more. We are also specialists in all Apple products, including MacBooks and iMacs.

How long will my repair take?

Our average turnaround time is 24 hours for most common repairs. More complex issues, like logic board repair or data recovery, may take longer. We'll give you a time estimate with your quote.
